Admin Guide

User Information Records

The user information records table shows the users' inputs retrieved from the face registration form. The table will update dynamically and automatically with new users' records.

Face Enrollment Processing

The "face enrollment processing" section enables admins to facilitate users in the face enrollment process.

You are required to perform the following steps:

  • You may choose to enter the number of records to enroll and process. For example, if 25 records were entered, the first 25 rows will be displayed upon clicking on the Generate button.
  • Click on the Reset button to revert back to the initial table.
  • You may choose to select multiple column(s) to enroll and process.
  • Ensure that the above steps are completed before proceeding to complete the face enrollment process.

Once the face enrollment process is completed, please proceed to click on "Proceed to train" button to train users' photos.
