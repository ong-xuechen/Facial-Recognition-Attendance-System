Photo Submission Requirements

Photo Submission Requirements

To complete your face registration, you will need to provide a recent photo of yourself for identification and facial recognition purposes. Do get yourself familiarized with the photo submission requirements to ensure that your photo(s) meet the necessary criteria. Some common requirements may include image formats (for example, jpg, jpeg, png), file size limitations, aspect ratio, background color, resolution, and any specific facial orientation or expression guidelines.

Photo submission options may include the following:

  • Uploading photo(s) from computer
  • Uploading photo(s) from mobile device
  • Submitting print photo(s)

Kindly be reminded to upload your face photo(s) at the "Photo Upload" section before proceeding to submit them for facial recognition processing.

About Photos

Below are some important guidelines to remember when capturing your face:

  • Ensure that your photo(s) display a clear image of ONLY you against a plain background; and it should not be blurry, grainy or fuzzy, etc.
  • Ensure that your photo(s) is/are a full face-and-shoulders shot, squarely facing the camera
  • Please use a portrait NOT landscape photo(s) that is/are correctly oriented
  • You are required to face the camera when capturing your face images
  • Avoid wearing dark sunglasses that will cover your eyes
  • Please do not include any enhancements to your photo(s), e.g., no lenses, filters, text, emoji, stickers, etc.
  • For individuals who are wearing a head covering daily for religious reasons, adjust the camera to provide a full-face view

User Guide on Face Registration

Face Registration Form

Upon login, navigate to the Face registration form page and you will noticed that there are the "User Information" and "Photo Upload" sections.

Uploading Photos

Formatting and Sizing

  • You may choose to upload a jpg, jpeg, png, jfif, bmp or other image file, whichever is preferred
  • Note that the maximum file size is 2GB
  • Should you plan to upload a scanned photo of your face portrait, please ensure that the resulting file is generated with 2 inch by 2 inch dimensions or larger
  • The image file should be at least 650 by 500 pixels
  • When scanning any photos, edit and crop the image to show ONLY your head and shoulders before uploading it

Uploading from computer

You will be given a prompt to upload your image when registering online. If you register using your personal mobile device, you may be able to directly upload a saved photo or take a photo directly from your device.

Upload photos

At the registration form page, click on "Upload files" displayed at the "Photo Upload" container. The file explorer or a dialogue box will appear, allowing you to navigate to the location of your prepared image file. Select the image file and click on the "Open" button to initiate the file transfer. To remove the photos, simply click on the "Cancel" button displayed at the bottom of registration form.

Review and Confirmation

After the photo file upload is completed, the "Photo Upload" section will display an image preview of the uploaded image file(s). Please carefully review the image preview while ensuring that your face is clearly visible, meets the specified requirements, and there should not be any distractions or obstructions in your photos. Once you are satisfied with the uploaded photos, click on the "Submit" button to proceed. Otherwise, click on the "Cancel" button to restart the registration process.

Confirmation and Completion

Upon successful submission, you will receive a confirmation message or notification indicating that your face registration has been successful. If required, you may be prompted to perform additional steps or provide further information. Please follw any remaining instructions provided by the registration form to finalize the registration process.

Congratulations! You have successfully completed the face registration form and your face photos will be processed by the administrators of the organization or service that you are registered with.
